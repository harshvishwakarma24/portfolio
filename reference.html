How Your Donation Works

The entire process is safe, simple, and takes less than an hour of your time.

1

Quick Health Screen

A private mini-physical to check your pulse, blood pressure, and hemoglobin levels to ensure you're fit to give.

2

The Donation

The actual blood draw takes only about 8-10 minutes. Relax, listen to music, or chat with our staff.

3

Refreshments

Enjoy free snacks and drinks for 15 minutes before heading out with the pride of knowing you saved a life.
